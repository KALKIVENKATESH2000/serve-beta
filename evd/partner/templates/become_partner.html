WHY become an eVidyaloka partner:


  • 85% of 1.3 million schools are in Villages
  • 50% of rural children drop-out between class V & VIII
  • 54% of Class 5 children can’t read standard 2 Text
  • 73% of Class 5 children can’t do a 2 digit Division
  • There are schools with 400 children and just 2 teachers
    • Starving to learn…
    • 1.2 million - the Teachers shortage in India !
  • Align your CSR goals to solve challenges in rural India



Outcomes:
  • Improved learning outcomes of enrolled children
  • Attendance in school and interaction with School teachers improves
Impacts:
  • Continuation of Education by students passing out of eVidyaloka
  • Children in Grade 5,6,7,8 in remote, rural villages are geared up to deal with future challenges and set higher life objectives
  • Improved Retention rate


How can you partner with eVidyaloka:
  • - Become a Delivery partner and start eVidyaloka classes in the school
  • - Become an Online Volunteering partner and have volunteers teach online


What is delivered:
  • Online Classes for Children studying in 5th -8th grades in government schools in rural villages and teachers allotted
  • Subjects Offered : English, Maths and Science
  • No. of Classes per week: 2 per subject, per week
  • Medium of instruction – In Local language
