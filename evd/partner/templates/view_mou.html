Memorandum of Understanding

 

I. Parties

         The purpose of this Memorandum of Understanding (“MOU”) is to establish a relationship between eVidyaloka Education for All Network Foundation, having its office at #10B 1 st Main Road MCSHColony, 5 th Block, HSR LAYOUT SECTOR 6 BANGALORE 560 102, herein referred to as ‘eVean Foundation’ and ‘{{partner_name_org}}’ with their address at {{partner_address}} herein referred to as ‘PARTNER’ dated {{acc_start_date}}.

         WHERE as eVean Foundation is a registered non profit Section 8 company that focuses on serving the rural students and transforming the educational landscape of rural India and eVean Foundation’s services or program described in this MOU will be provided to school/students mutually agreed between eVean Foundation and PARTNER.

    WHERE as PARTNER is an individual with an intention to transform rural India through technology and entrepreneurship and is interested in availing the eVean Foundation program to the mutually agreed schools/students.

         BOTH PARTIES HEREBY enter into this MOU to enable eVean Foundation and PARTNER to provide said services/program to school(s) (“Schools”) covered in the Terms of Engagement added as Exhibit 2 of the MOU.

II. Payments

PARTNER herewith acknowledges that Payments will be as agreed under the Scope & Budget Plan appearing in the Scope & Budget added as Exhibit 1 of the MOU.

Payments will be made as a grant to eVean Foundation.

III. Duration

         This MOU is for a period of {{total_month}} months from {{acc_start_date}} to {{academic_year_end}}.

IV. Hold Harmless/Indemnity

         Both eVean Foundation and PARTNER are independent enterprises and shall deal at arm’s length and shall not commit on each other’s behalf without the express concurrence of the other.

         Each party to this MOU agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the other for and from any claims, causes of action, or any other proceeding of any type or kind that is made against the other where such claim, cause of action or other proceeding arises from the conduct, act, omission, or commission by the other party.

V. Exit/Renewal

          Either party may terminate this MOU at any time, without cause, with 30 days written notice to the other party. Upon termination, both parties will work to close within 30 days all pending matters and financial obligations. PARTNER will return or destroy all confidential information as directed by eVean Foundation

         Both parties will honour the confidentiality and data protection commitment even after termination and the same commitment shall survive indefinitely.

         This engagement may be renewed and/or amended by mutual consent of the parties.  All amendments must be in writing and signed by both the parties.

VI. Governing Law

      The MOU shall be governed by the Indian Laws for the time being in force and till the period of satisfactory performance of the MOU.

         The Courts of Bengaluru alone shall have exclusive jurisdiction in respect of any matter, claim or dispute arising out of or in relation to this MOU.
