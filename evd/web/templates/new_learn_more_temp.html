eVidyaloka strives to brighten the futures of rural children and uplift communities through digital classrooms conducted by volunteer teachers across the world.

How it works

• Remote volunteer Teachers are sourced
• A Remote Class Admin is assigned
• Timetable, Curriculum planned, Students enrolled,   Teachers allotted
• On-boarded on My eVidyaloka Technology Platform
• A local partner is engaged
• Broadband connectivity is obtained
• Government is informed by partner
• Digital Classroom setup in rural Govt School
• A Local Class Assistant to Operate
