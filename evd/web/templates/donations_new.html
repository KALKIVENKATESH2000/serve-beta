Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does Educate One Rural Child option mean ?
    • This is the operating cost involved in connecting a volunteer teacher to the child in the village school. Typical costs include - Broadband expenses, Classroom resource person, Backend coordinators, Technology Platform costs etc. A child gets 2 classes per week per subject - Maths, Science and English, for the entire academic year
  • What does Adopting a Digital Classroom mean ?
    • This will help establishing the Digital Classroom setup in an identified village school, through which online classes are conducted by the volunteers to the school children. The cost goes towards the equipments - Computer, LED TV Screen, Camera, UPS and Modem
  • What does Sponsor a full Center mean ?
    • This helps in the complete setup of the Digital Classroom and the running/operating cost of the center with an approximate strength of 45 Children, for the entire year.
  • Are my donations, eligible for Income Tax exemptions ?
    • Yes, donations made to eVidyaloka Trust are eligble for tax exemptions under Section 80g of Income Tax act

  • How does money transfer
    happen ?
    • Once a donation option is selected, on clicking 'Donate', essential information is captured in the next screen. A donor can then pay instantly through 'Online' option. Or use his/her NET Banking, by adding eVidyaloka account details and do a NEFT transfer. Alternately, payment can also be madey via 'Cheque' with information provided and mail to the eVidyaloka Communication address as specified at www.evidyaloka.org/contact_us. This NEFT/Cheque information can be update back in portal, with the login credentials that is mailed to you.
  • Do I get receipts for the Sponsorships made and when ?
    • Soft copies of Donations receipts will be issued within 1 Week on receipt of funds. And physical copy can be posted to the given address, on an email request.
  • Do you accept donations in USD ?
    • No, eVidyaloka at this moment can accept donations only in Indian Rupees.
  • For Digital classroom adoption: Will I know the specific centre details ?
    • Yes, We are shortlisting the centre locations and you will be communicated the specific Centre information, once the engagement is finalized. You will also receive period updates regarding the Centre.
  • For Child sponsorships: Will I know the specific Child(ren) details ?
    • eVidyaloka targets an optimal strength of 45 students per Centre. As we work with Govt. School children, We may not be able to share specific child details, as this is the generic class room operating cost, for this classroom strength.
    • However, if your sponsorship is for a Full class (15 children), we will communicate to you, the associated Centre details.
