About

Event Brief

Redefining Equality and e-Quality in education is the central theme of RUBARU: a rural-urban children connect event that is being organized by eVidyaloka. With 250+ participative audience, the occasion is being celebrated with a joint cultural performance by five schools from cities and villages across India. As a run-up to this event, a series of online student exchanges are being conducted in the participating schools, cherishing the spirit of Joy of Giving (Daan Utsav -2016).

On this occasion, a powerful panel of eminent academicians and thought leaders from leading institutions are being invited to share their views, perspectives and outlook on the ‘Digital highway to bridge the Rural-Urban education divide’. With a high degree of participation from an audience, that includes, Decision makers, Teachers, Educators, Parents, Volunteers, Patrons the event is envisaged to be a catalyst for the cause of nation building through education.
