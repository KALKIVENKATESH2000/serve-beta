Volunteer Onboarding FAQ :

1. I didn't receive the Activation link in my email, what do I do ?

- Please check your SPAM folder of the mail id provided

- The email entered, may have typographical errors. Please try registering again, with the correct email address


2. What do I do after Account activation ?

- Please login to your account and update your profile to initiate the volunteer onboarding process. Please do select the respective reference channel ( Corporate- Brillio ).

- The onboarding process for Teacher role involve 5 steps as below

- For other roles, you will reached out to by an eVidyaloka rep, on updation of the profile


3. What are the next steps to volunteer, on Signing up ?

- The onboarding process for Teacher role involve 5 steps as below


4. Whom do I reach out to for any queries on Volunteer Onboarding ?

- After logging in, certain standard queries are answered in the FAQ section of Onboarding, which is under My Profile > Onboarding Info

- You can simply any email communication you would have received during the course of onboarding

- You can reach out to your local point of contact, in case of Corporate employee volunteers

- You could also write to onboarding@evidyaloka.org


5. Why do I see two different user interface experience, one while browsing the website and another after logging in?

- We are in the process of refreshing our overall website and as a first iteration, have released the static components of the website. We will soon be refreshing the logged in mode of the interface as well, which is currently under development.
