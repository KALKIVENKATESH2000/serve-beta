Overview

Overview : Rubaru is the annual summit by eVidyaloka, promoting the spirit of rural-urban connection. This is the third edition of the event, where thought leaders, from across the industry come together on a social discussion platform, to engage and discuss with other industry leaders, CxOs, CSR heads and business leaders and share thoughts and insights.

The Purpose of Rubaru:

eVidyaloka is keen to create and celebrate the combined determination of individual impacting the rural education system through urban support. We focus is oneVidyaloka is keen to create and celebrate the combined determination of individual impacting the rural education system through urban support. Our focus is on:

How can urban development impact the rural education system in India?

How are educational institutes looking at the governance, and impact of technology to scale?

How does one build a future-ready system by multiple stakeholders participating in solutions?

Key Themes

To understand and implement the format of volunteering to teach and involving individuals in the larger national purpose.
