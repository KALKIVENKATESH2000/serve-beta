Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does Money Transfer happen?
    • Once the Donation option is selected by clicking on 'Donate', essential information is captured in the next screen. A donor can then pay instantly through the 'Online' option or use Net Banking by adding eVidyaloka Account Details and do an NEFT transfer. Alternately, payment can also be made via cheque with the information provided and mail to the eVidyaloka Communication address, as specified on www.evidyaloka.org/contact_us. The NEFT/Cheque information would be updated in the portal, along with the log-in credentials that would be mailed to you.
  • Do I get receipts for the Sponsorships made, and when?
    • Soft copies of Donations receipts will be issued within a week of donations received. Physical copy can also be posted to the given address, on an email request.
  • Do you accept donations in USD?
    • No, currently eVidyaloka can accept donations only in Indian Rupees.
  • For Digital Classroom adoption, will I know the specific centre details?
    • Yes, you will be communicated the specific Centre information, but only after the formalities of the adoption are completed. You will also receive periodic updates regarding the Centre.
  • For Child Sponsorships, will I know the details of the specific child(ren)?
    • eVidyaloka targets an optimal strength of 45 students per Centre. As we work with govt. school children, we may not be able to share specific child details, as this is the generic classroom operating cost, for the respective classroom strength.
    • However, if your sponsorship is for an entire class (15 children) we will share the respective Centre’s details with you.
  • Where can I know more about eVidyaloka model details?

    • Please visit our General FAQ

      section to know more.
